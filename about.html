About

Home > About

Sankalp Digital Path to Success is an online education platform that provides high-quality study materials for students. We offer notes, previous year questions (PYQs), important questions, model papers, syllabus, and topic-wise summaries to support effective learning and exam preparation.

Our goal is to make learning simple, accessible, and student-friendly by providing well-organized and updated academic resources.

Mission & Vision

We observed that many students depend on outdated PYQs and notes borrowed from seniors or other sources, which often creates confusion and affects exam preparation. To solve this problem, we decided to provide latest PYQs, updated notes, and reliable study materials in one place.

Our mission is to support students with accurate, exam-oriented, and easy-to-understand content, helping them prepare with confidence and clarity.

What We Offer

Sankalp Digital Path to Success offers:

  • Updated PYQs
  • Subject-wise notes
  • Important questions
  • Model question papers
  • Syllabus
  • Topic-wise summaries

All materials are prepared to match the CBSE syllabus and exam pattern.

Target Audience

Sankalp Digital Path to Success is specially designed forClass 9 to Class 12 CBSE students who want clear concepts and reliable study resources for board exam preparation.

Free Learning Platform

This is a completely free education platform. Students can easily download notes, PYQs, model papers, syllabus, and important questions for each subject without any cost.
